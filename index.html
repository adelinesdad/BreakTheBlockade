Welcome to Break the Blockade!

The Red King is under siege by the Blue Army. The Red Battalions must reach their King to provide the kingdom with provisions to outlast the seige.

Red wins if:

  • One of the red pieces reaches the King piece (piece with the double black border) within 10 turns.

Blue wins if:

  • No red piece reache the King piece withing ten turns, or
  • All red pieces have been captured or immobilized

Movement rules:

  • Pawns can't move into or through spaces occupied by other pieces, except for Red pieces that can move onto the King piece to win the game.
  • Red:
    • Red moves first.
    • Red can move a total of 3 spaces on each turn, distributed among 1, 2, or 3 pieces.
    • The King piece can only move within the Castle (the center gray area)
  • Blue:
    • Blue can move only 1 piece, and can only move it 1 space.
    • Blue pieces cannot move into the Castle

Immobility: If a single red piece is adjacent to a single blue piece which is not adjacent to another other red piece, the red piece cannot move.

Release: If a single blue piece is adjacent to 2+ red pieces, all the red pieces can move.

Capture: If 2 blue pieces are both adjacent to a single red piece, the red piece is captured and removed from the board.

How to use this web-based simulator:

  • Click a piece to select it
  • Then click the location you want to move it to
  • Due to technical limitations, you can only move a piece in a straight line. However, if you want the piece to turn, just move it to one space, then click it again and move it again in a different direction.
  • The panel above will indicate which player's turn it is, how many moves they have left, and how many rounds remain.
